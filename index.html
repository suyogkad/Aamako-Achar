Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long do your pickles last?

Our pickles can last up to 12 months when stored in a cool, dry place.

2. Do you use any preservatives?

No, our pickles are made with natural ingredients and do not contain any artificial preservatives.

3. Are your pickles gluten-free?

Yes, all our pickles are gluten-free.

4. Can I order pickles in bulk?

Definitely, please contact us directly for bulk orders.

5. Are there any allergens in your pickles?

Please check the label for specific allergen information.

Our pickles are packaged in glass jars to preserve freshness and flavor.

We accept returns within 14 days of purchase.
